Now the game is only avaliable on AppStore. We are not planning to bring it to other platforms. But maybe we will in the future.

How to play the game

You control a robot called Alan by entering special commands. Alan is able to distinguish colors around himself and to move in four directions. You will be guided through the first few levels. To enter a command you should model a situation. For example, if the robot reaches a block with a red side on the left, then he should move up. As a result you guide Alan to collect stars on a level. You may change commands' order by dragging it to the left or right. You may remove a command by dragging it down.

My game has stopped working

Turn your iPad off and on, and then open Robot Alan again.
