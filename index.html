RobotAlan - is a puzzle in the full sense of the word. It is not an easy game and therefore it is not for everyone, but for those who like to think. You will control a robot called Alan by entering special commands. Alan is able to distinguish colors around himself and to move in four directions. Based on this data you will lead him through a variety of levels.

To enter a command you should model a situation. For example, if the robot reaches a block with a red side on the left, then he should move up. As a result you guide Alan to collect stars on a level. You may change commands' order by dragging it to the left or right. You may remove a command by dragging it down.
